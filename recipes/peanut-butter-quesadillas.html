Peanut Butter Quesadillas

Description

Peanut butter quesadillas are a delightful twist on a classic comfort food. This simple yet satisfying dish combines the creamy richness of peanut butter with the warm, crispy texture of a tortilla. Whether you enjoy them plain or with a touch of sweetness, peanut butter quesadillas are a quick and easy meal or snack that's sure to satisfy your cravings.

Perfect for a quick breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a late-night snack, peanut butter quesadillas are a versatile and delicious treat. You can customize them to your liking by adding toppings like sliced bananas, chocolate chips, or a drizzle of honey. With their simple ingredients and easy preparation, peanut butter quesadillas are a must-try for anyone who loves comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 (10 inch) flour tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons grape jelly

Steps

Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat. Spread one side of the tortilla with peanut butter. Fold tortilla in half so that the peanut butter is on the inside. Place folded tortilla in the skillet, and heat 2 minutes on each side, until lightly browned. Slice into wedges, and dip in the jelly to serve.
